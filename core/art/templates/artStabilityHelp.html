ART GRID jobs help

The stability view shows comparison of test results with regard to previous nightly.

The color coding of the comparison:
  • active
    - PanDA job still running.
  • na
    - there is no previous test to compare with
  • ok
    - Athena and sub-steps of both current and previous tests are succeeded;
  • warning
    - Athena step of both tests succeeded, but the same sub-steps failed;
  • warning_b
    - Athena step of both tests succeeded, but the current test went further i.e. at least one of sub-steps failed which is farther than failed sub-step of the previous nightly test;
  • warning_w
    - Athena step of both tests succeeded, but at least one sub-step of the current test failed sooner than the failed sub-step of the previous nightly test;
  • alert
    - Athena step or PanDA job failed.
